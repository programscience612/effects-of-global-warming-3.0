History of the Buzzer Wire Game

Invented in 1953 by Robert (Bob) Scrimshaw in Yorkshire, England, for a garden party. It was patented by John Waddington and became popular in the mid-1950s.

Bob was an RAF electrician during WWII, and the game started as the 'electric loop game'.
